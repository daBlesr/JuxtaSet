Upload Your Own Dataset

On this page you are enabled to upload your own dataset. Note a few things however. This upoad page does not support upload of large datasets, you will get a timeout error. JuxtaSet will only import large datasets using command line in the future. Second, do NOT upload sensitive data. JuxtaSet is not secure, other people may be able to access your data.

CSV files can be uploaded that are delimited with semicolons ; and quoted with ". Example CSV file: Sample.txt
Note if you would like to visualize the temporal attribute, you will need to copy the column just like in the sample document.
